Style guide

Brand Overview

The Ferrari brand represents a legacy of luxury, speed, and Italian craftsmanship. This website aims to create an immersive experience for car enthusiasts, luxury buyers, and Ferrari aficionados. The tone of voice will be energetic and aspirational, capturing the passion and thrill associated with the Ferrari brand. Our goal is to not only showcase the cars but also to foster a community among users who share a deep love for these iconic vehicles.

Color Palette

The color palette is designed to enhance the website’s modern, sleek aesthetic while aligning with Ferrari’s iconic style. The primary color, deep black (0, 0, 0), conveys elegance and power, while the gradient of grays—from dark (50, 49, 49) to very light gray (217, 217, 217)—creates contrast and depth across each page. These shades provide a sophisticated visual balance, allowing imagery and interactive elements to stand out effectively.

Typography

Typography is central to establishing the site’s tone and user experience. Poppins, a modern sans-serif font, has been chosen as the primary typeface due to its clean lines and contemporary style, which perfectly match Ferrari’s ethos of elegance and precision. Its balanced proportions and readability make it ideal for both headings and body text, allowing users to navigate the site with ease. This typographic approach not only reinforces the brand’s visual identity but also ensures a cohesive and visually pleasing experience across all devices and screen sizes.

UI Elements

The user interface will be designed for intuitive navigation and visual appeal. The navigation bar will feature a white background with links in Black for high visibility.

Imagery and Graphics

High-quality imagery is essential to capture the essence of Ferrari. Car images will be bright and vibrant, showcasing the sleek design and unique features of each vehicle. A consistent aspect ratio will be maintained across the gallery and catalog for uniformity.

Layout and Grid System

To create a structured and visually appealing layout, a single-row grid system will be used, displaying content in distinct columns. This approach allows for clear alignment and spacing across all screen sizes, ensuring consistency without the need for complex adjustments

Interactive Elements

Interactive elements will enhance user engagement. Subtle animations will be used for buttons and links, providing immediate feedback during user interactions. Transition effects for images and graphs will create a smooth experience as users navigate through the site. On the homepage, a dynamic animation will introduce key aspects of the Ferrari brand, setting an exciting tone for the rest of the site.

Accessibility Considerations

To ensure the website is accessible to all users, a strong focus on color contrast will be implemented. All text will be legible against background colors, adhering to WCAG guidelines
