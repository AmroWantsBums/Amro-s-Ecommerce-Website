Style guide

Brand Overview

The Ferrari brand represents a legacy of luxury, speed, and Italian craftsmanship. This website aims to create an immersive experience for car enthusiasts, luxury buyers, and Ferrari aficionados. The tone of voice will be energetic and aspirational, capturing the passion and thrill associated with the Ferrari brand. Our goal is to not only showcase the cars but also to foster a community among users who share a deep love for these iconic vehicles.

Color Palette

The color palette will enhance the website’s aesthetic while reflecting the essence of Ferrari. The primary color will be Ferrari Red (#FF2800), symbolizing the brand's passion and performance. Various shades of red will be used to create depth and visual interest:

  • Primary Color: Ferrari Red (#FF2800)
  • Dark Red: (#B22222) for highlights and backgrounds
  • Light Red: (#FF6F61) for secondary accents
  • Black: (#000000) for all text to ensure readability
  • White: (#FFFFFF) for contrast and to separate sections visually

Typography

Typography will play a crucial role in establishing the site’s tone. We will use Poppins, a modern sans-serif font that conveys elegance and clarity. This will be backed up with sans-serif to fall back on if Poppins is not available.

UI Elements

The user interface will be designed for intuitive navigation and visual appeal. The navigation bar will feature a white background with links in Black for high visibility.

Imagery and Graphics

High-quality imagery is essential to capture the essence of Ferrari. Car images will be bright and vibrant, showcasing the sleek design and unique features of each vehicle. A consistent aspect ratio will be maintained across the gallery and catalog for uniformity. For data visualizations, a Bubble Word Map will display car names in varying shades of red, with clear and readable bubbles. On hover, an overlay will show the car's image, price, name, and fuel type. A Line Graph will illustrate price trends over the past four years, with data points in Ferrari Red and connecting lines in Dark Red, featuring clear Black axis labels for easy interpretation.

Layout and Grid System

To achieve a structured and visually appealing layout, a 3-column grid system will be employed. This flexible design allows for easy adjustments across different screen sizes while maintaining alignment and spacing. Responsive design is crucial for users on various devices. On smaller screens, the navigation bar will collapse into a hamburger menu, ensuring a clean and accessible experience.

Interactive Elements

Interactive elements will enhance user engagement. Subtle animations will be used for buttons and links, providing immediate feedback during user interactions. Transition effects for images and graphs will create a smooth experience as users navigate through the site. On the homepage, a dynamic animation will introduce key aspects of the Ferrari brand, setting an exciting tone for the rest of the site.

Accessibility Considerations

To ensure the website is accessible to all users, a strong focus on color contrast will be implemented. All text will be legible against background colors, adhering to WCAG guidelines
